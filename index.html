Our Team!

Target,Tackle and together we aim to solve the real hunger, We are a non-profit enabling the exchange of food from the food industry to those who actually need it.

What we do?

We'll provide the technology infrastructure to enable real-time food donation.

How we do?

We target the places and people who actually need food,associate with all forms who can deliver the food and provide them to people in need.

The Three D's we look so far!

To end hunger and malnutrition, and to ensure that everyone has access to safe, nutritious, and sufficient food

The world produces enough food to feed all of its 8 billion people, yet 733 million people (1 in 11) go hungry every day.We are here to figure that demand.

It is a practical experience. When we are hungry, nature will force us to try to fulfil our hunger.We are here to devote ourselfs to their hunger.

Hunger is a widespread problem in the entire country, which creates a vicious cycle of poverty, low economic growth, and health concerns.Lets create a helping cycle for Hunger,together.
